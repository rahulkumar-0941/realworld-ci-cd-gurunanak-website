About Us

Mr. Upendra Singh

Founder & Managing Director

Founded in 2010 by Mr. Upendra Singh, Shree Gurunanak Transport Company has become a trusted and well-known name in India’s logistics sector. With roots grounded in experience from an early age, Mr. Singh turned his lifelong understanding of the transport industry into a full-scale logistics solution connecting truck owners with goods providers across the country.


From humble beginnings to becoming a respected logistics partner, our mission has always been clear — to build a pan-India transport network that’s accessible online and known for its reliability, reach, and customer satisfaction. Today, our presence is recognized and respected in states like Bihar, Jharkhand, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Chhattisgarh, Uttar Pradesh, Karnataka, Punjab, Haryana, Delhi, Gujarat, West Bengal, and even Guwahati.


Shree Gurunanak Transport Company is known for its clean, transparent, and dependable service. Our unique strength lies in maintaining strong relationships with both truck owners and goods providers, ensuring fast, safe, and seamless transportation. We take pride in being the first choice for logistics needs — whether you're moving goods across the state or across the nation.


With a reputation built on integrity, efficiency, and reliability, we continue to evolve with technology while holding firmly to the values that made us a household name in transport. Our commitment to customer satisfaction and operational excellence drives us every day, as we work toward connecting every corner of India through better logistics.


Shree Gurunanak Transport Company is your trusted logistics partner, connecting truck owners and goods owners seamlessly across India. We pride ourselves on reliability, safety, and exceptional customer service.
